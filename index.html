Heart Attack Analysis and Prediction Dataset

This dataset contains information about people and heart attack risk.

APA citation

Overview

With the dataset provided for heart analysis, I will analyse the possibilities of heart attack on the these features: Age, Gender, Resting Blood Pressure, Cholesterol, and Fasting Blood Sugar. That means I will use data from 165 people who are at high risk and figure out the relationship between these characteristics and the high risk of heart attack.

Questions to be answered:

1. Does the age of a person contribute towards heart attack?

2. Does gender (male/female) increase the risk of heart attack?

3. Are the resting blood pressure, cholesterol, and fasting blood sugar related to the high risk of heart attack?
